# EPA Emission Guidelines

1. Passenger Cars: Average emissions are approximately 0.404 kg CO2e per mile.
2. Diet: A plant-based diet can reduce a person's food footprint by up to 50% compared to a high-meat diet.
3. Electricity: Natural gas plants emit about 0.42 kg CO2e per kWh, while solar and wind emit less than 0.05 kg CO2e per kWh over their lifecycle.
